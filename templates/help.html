Help

How to use Pickr: Pickr lets you quickly create and join polls. Create a poll: Click “Start a new poll”, enter your question and at least two answer options, then start the poll. You will receive a unique code. Join a poll: Click “Join a poll” and enter the code to access the poll. Vote: Select one of the available options to cast your vote. View results: After voting, you can see the results with percentages and bars. Share your code with others so they can join and vote!
